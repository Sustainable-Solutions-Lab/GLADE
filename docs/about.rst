About
=====

GLADE is currently developed at the `Sustainable Solutions Lab <https://sustainablesolutions.stanford.edu/>`_ at Stanford University. It is primarily built by `Koen van Greevenbroek <https://sustainablesolutions.stanford.edu/people/koen-van-greevenbroek>`_, with helpful advice and feedback from `Ken Caldeira <https://sustainablesolutions.stanford.edu/people/ken-caldeira>`_ and `Steven J. Davis <https://sustainablesolutions.stanford.edu/people/steven-davis>`_.
