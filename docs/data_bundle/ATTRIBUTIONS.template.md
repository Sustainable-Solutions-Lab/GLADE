## Required attributions

### IHME Global Burden of Disease

Files derived from IHME GBD data (`health/gbd_mortality_rates.csv`,
`health/relative_risks.csv`, `diet/gbd_dietary_risk_exposure.csv`)
must carry the following source identifier verbatim in any
publication that uses them:

> Source: Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation.
> Used with permission. All rights reserved.

Per-file citations:

- **Mortality (GBD 2023)**: Global Burden of Disease Collaborative
  Network. *Global Burden of Disease Study 2023 (GBD 2023) Results*.
  Seattle, United States: Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation
  (IHME), 2025. Available from https://vizhub.healthdata.org/gbd-results/.
- **Relative risks (GBD 2019)**: Global Burden of Disease Collaborative
  Network. *Global Burden of Disease Study 2019 (GBD 2019) Results*.
  Seattle, United States: Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation
  (IHME), 2020.
- **Dietary risk exposure (GBD 2019)**: Global Burden of Disease
  Collaborative Network. *Global Burden of Disease Study 2019 (GBD 2019)
  Dietary Risk Exposure Estimates 1990-2019*. Seattle, United States:
  Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation (IHME), 2021.

### Global Dietary Database (Tufts University)

Files derived from the GDD (`diet/gdd_dietary_intake.csv`) must
acknowledge GDD and Tufts University in any downstream publication:

> Global Dietary Database. Dietary intake data by country.
> https://www.globaldietarydatabase.org/ [Accessed during food-opt
> bundle build {{BUILD_DATE}}].

The GDD's terms of use prohibit redistribution of the original data
sets and any sale of derivative works. This bundle re-distributes
processed derivatives only, in good faith reliance on the GDD's
clause permitting "use in non-commercial products or services" with
attribution. See the food-opt repository's correspondence record for
the permission status. Commercial use of this bundle is forbidden by
both CC BY-NC 4.0 and the GDD terms.
